Conforming Fixed Rate Mortgages

This is a traditional loan that has a stable and low interest rate, but may or may not include a PMI payment in addition.

Income, credit, and asset qualifications for this type of loan are strict and the loan amounts are restricted depending on the number of units in the subject property.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Good to excellent credit (usually 620 or above)
  • 2 years at the same job
  • 3 months of reserve income (in addition to down payment)
  • Low debt to income ratio
  • Property specific (contact loan officer for more information)

Maximum Amount: $333,700

Situations to consider this type of loan:

  • Home buyer with at least 10% of the purchase price available for down payment, intent to live in the home.
  • Investment property purchase with same qualifications as above except at least 15% of purchase price avaliable for down payment.
  • Refinance existing mortgage with a loan amount equal to or less than 80% of the home's value
  • Refinance existing mortgage in conjunction with a second mortgage
