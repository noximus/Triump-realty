Getting The Loan That Works For You.
At Triumph, we've cut through the red tape. That's why we can prequalify you in as little as 24 hours and close your loan in days, not months. Here's how it happens:


1. Contact a Triumph Personal Mortgage Specialist to discuss your needs.
• Fill out our quick prequalification form
(you will be contacted in as little as 24 hours)
OR
• Call your local branch


2. Your Personal Mortgage Specialist will contact you to assess your situation and determine your financial goals. Expect to discuss the following information: estimated value of your home and your credit history.


3. Based on the information collected and your credit report, your Personal Mortgage
Specialist will customize a loan amount which is tailored to your specific situation.


4. Your Personal Mortgage Specialist will request financial documents , set an appointment for an appraiser to assess the value of your home, and even do all the necessary paperwork for you.


5. With your application now complete, your Personal Mortgage Specialist will notify you when your loan is approved and your loan papers are ready to be signed. You'll receive your funds approximately one week after signing.
