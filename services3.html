Conforming Adjustable Rate Mortgages

This is a traditional loan that has a very low interest rate that would be fixed for 1, 3, 5, or 7 years, and may or may not include a PMI payment in addition.

Income, credit, and asset qualifications for this type of loan are strict and the loan amounts are restricted depending on the number of units in the subject property.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Good to excellent credit (usually 620 or above)
  • 2 years at the same job
  • 3 months of reserve income (in addition to down payment)
  • Low debt to income ratio
  • Property specific (contact loan officer for more information)


Term: 30 years  

Situations to consider this type of loan are similar to the Fixed Conforming Loan with these exceptions:

  • Property buyer who requires a lower monthly payment than the fixed rate will allow.
  • Buyer who intends to own the property for a short set amount of time (usually 1, 3, 5, or 7 years).
